Hello there, and welcome to my homepage!

My name is Aritra Chakrabarty , and I am an Indian Astrophysicist working as a Postdoc at the Data Observatory and Universidad Adolfo Ibáñez in Santiago, Chile. My research primarily focuses on bridging the formation and evolution pathways of small exoplanets with their observed demographics. My work involves statistical modeling and numerical simulations. I also work on radiative transfer in atmospheres of exoplanets and brown dwarfs using numerical techniques. Utilizing these models, I generate captivating simulations of spectra, phase curves, and polarization observable from such astronomical objects.

Visit the research page to know more about my research projects and see some interesting simulations. You may also check out my Github profile.

To get a better insight on my background, you can have a look at my curriculum vitae.
